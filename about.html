About Me

Hello! I am Colleen Higgins and I love knitting.

When did you start knitting?

I learned how to knit when I was 8 or 9 years old, though I didn't get very good until high school. My mom taught me the knit stitch and then I just ran with it, finding free patterns and then learning whatever skills I needed in order to complete it. Eventually I started changing patterns around and building my own.

Why is knitting special to you?

The idea of knitting is so powerful. You start with a fairly useless and (honestly) boring pile of yarn and with some creativity and work you can end up with a beautiful and functional piece of art. Knitting has become a part of my identity; when a friend or stranger compliments my scarf or asks where I bought my hat, I get a rush of pride and a sense of accomplishment.I keep my knitting with me at pretty much all times. When I'm watching, I always pull out my knitting. If I'm waiting for the subway, I try to get a few rows in. My phone is filled with pictures of instructions and notes on patterns I'm working on.

How would you describe the designs you make?

My goal is to make functional, wearable art that is fun to knit and comfortable to wear. I write my patterns so that they are easily understandable and I try to provide as many resources as possible so that the knitting process can be relaxing and enjoyable.
